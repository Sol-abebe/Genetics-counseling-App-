Recurrence parameters for free Trisomy 21 are bounded by a 1% threshold or age risk limits.

📋 Action Protocols & Preventative Tracks

  • First-Trimester Screening (10-14 Weeks): Serum PAPP-A, free β-hCG, and nuchal translucency ultrasound markers.
  • Cell-Free DNA (cfDNA/NIPT): High sensitivity tracking (>99% for T21) accessible from gestation week 10 onwards.
  • Ultrasound Soft Indicators: Increased nuchal fold thickness, absent nasal bone, or complex endocardial cushion defects.
  • Primary Driver: Chromosomal non-disjunction caused by spindle degradation errors, heavily correlated with advanced maternal age.
